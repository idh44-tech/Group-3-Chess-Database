# Description

Our system is a website that allows users to create, update, and view information on chess players.  The site will track values such as elo, winrates, and common openings played.  It will also include useful miscellaneous usefule features such as a page providing information on various chess openings.  The site is targeted primarily towards players who play mostly in-person and don't have access to the information an playing online provides.
